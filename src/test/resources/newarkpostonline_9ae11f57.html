Man takes cash, iPhone from woman's jacket at Klondike Kate's

  • By Karie Simmons ksimmons@chespub.com
  • 7 hrs ago
  • 1
  • PHOTO COURTESY OF NPD

Police are trying to identify this man who stole cash, a debit card and an iPhone 7 out of woman's jacket at Klondike Kate's on March 11.

  • PHOTO COURTESY OF NPD

Police are trying to identify this man who stole cash, a debit card and an iPhone 7 out of woman's jacket at Klondike Kate's on March 11.

A man was captured on camera taking items out of a woman's jacket while it was hung on a coat rack at Klondike Kate's last weekend.

According to Newark Police spokesman Lt. Fred Nelson, the theft occurred at approximately 12:13 a.m. March 11 at the bar and restaurant located at 158 E. Main St.

The two victims, a 22-year-old woman and a 23-year-old woman, told police they placed a cellphone and their wallets inside of one of their jackets and hung the jacket on a coat rack on the second floor. When they returned for their items, they realized the jacket was missing.

Nelson said the women found the discarded jacket on the first floor, but $45 in cash, a debit card and a pink iPhone 7 were missing from the pockets.

NPD has released surveillance photos of the suspect taking the jacket from the coat rack and is asking for the public's help to identify him. He is described only as a man between 18 and 25 years old with a thin beard.

Anyone with any additional information about this incident or the suspect should contact Officer Matthew Coughlin at 302-366-7100 ext. 3143 or Matthew.Coughlin@cj.state.de.us.
