Tata TaMo Racemo sports car India launch likely in December

TaMo RaceMo sports car from Tata Motors will India’s first middle engine sports car which is likely to launch in India by December this year.

By Charanpreet Singh | Updated: March 28, 2017 7:30 PM IST
Email
Comments

TaMo, Tata Motors sub-brand showcased its first sports car at 2017 Geneva Motor Show this month which soon grabbed the eye balls of auto enthusiast not only in India but also globally. Christened as TaMo RaceMo, it is Tata’s first ever mid-engined sports car which is expected to launch in India by the end of this year. Moreover, the sports car will be produced in limited numbers and each produced vehicle will be numbered as seen on the pre-production models showcased at Geneva Auto Show with 001 and 002 numbers. The company also used the platform to showcase Tata Tigor compact sedan and Tata Nexon compact SUV alongside RaceMo sports car. Aimed to change the way India drives, the new RaceMo will be fully connected sports coupe with MMS technology. Also Read: TAMO Racemo from Tata Motors to comes in kits at dealerships

Talking more about the sports car, Tamo RaceMo will measure length of 3835mm, height of 1208mm and width of 1810mm along with wheelbase and ground clearance of 2430mm and 165mm. As we previously mentioned that TaMo RaceMo will be country’s first kit car which will be built on MOFlex Multi-Material Sandwich (MMS) platform. Moreover, it will be made with small number modules which will help the dealers and DIY enthusiasts to assemble the car easily.

Powering the TaMo RaceMo is a modified 1.2-litre Revotron turbocharged 3-cylinder engine which is assembled in the middle of the chassis. This petrol mill is capable of producing 190 PS of max power against the peak torque of 210 Nm. The engine comes mated to a 6-speed automated manual gearbox which sends power to the rear wheels. As far as the performance is considered, the RaceMo is capable of attaining three figure-mark (100 kmph) under 6 seconds. As a reminder, Tata Motors is all set to launch Tata Tigor compact sedan in India tomorrow. With an aim to reclaim the sub-compact segment, Tata Motors seems to be quite confident with its latest entrant which will be a volume dragger like Tiago hatchback. Bagging design elements along with interior equipments from Tiago, the compact sedan does get an overall distinctive look. It comes powered by 1.2-litre petrol engine as well as 1.05-litre diesel engine like-wise Tiago hatchback. Both the engines will be mated to a 5-speed manual gearbox ditching the AMT unit as of now. Likely to be priced in the range of INR 4.5 lakh – INR 6 lakh (ex-showroom), will Tata’s newest entrant be able to shatter the dominance of Maruti Dzire and Hyundai Xcent, well it will be answered eventually.
